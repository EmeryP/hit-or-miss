===============================================================

Hit or miss! is based on the classic game of Battleship and uses 
standard HTTP METHODS (GET/POST) to play the game.

Codefellowia is under attack and your naval armada is the only
thing standing between success and mid-term project failure.

Your mission: Destroy the advancing enemy naval fleet before 
they destroy you!

Hit or Miss is a turn based game alternating between you and 
your opponent.  Because of your superior experience, you will
start the game by placing the ships in your armada around your 
PRIMARY playing board. Instructions for placing your ships can 
be found at: GET hitormiss.fun/setup/help.

You will attack the enemy by shooting your missiles at their ships.  Targeting instructions can be found at:
GET hitormiss.fun/play/help.
